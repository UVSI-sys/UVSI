About UVSI

UVSI began as a small research group inside a university tech lab. The founders believed that visual intelligence should be trustworthy, auditable, and useful — not just novel. Over the years we evolved into an engineering-first company focusing on reproducible deployments, field maintenance, and clear ROI for customers.

Our approach blends rigorous testing, human-centered design, and modular hardware to deliver systems that last. Whether it’s helping a manufacturer reduce rework or enabling transit agencies to detect hazards faster, our mission is to improve outcomes using visual intelligence.
